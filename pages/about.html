About

Early Life

Born and raised by the San Francisco River in the countryside of Brazil, I have been learning how to fix and build machines for as long as I can remember. My most recent build is a multi-GPU desktop optimized to train deep learning models.

Education

As a mathematics undergraduate from the Montes Claros State University (Unimontes) in Brazil, I had my first experience in software development. As a mathematics graduate from Pontificia Catholic University in Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio), I wrote my thesis on Differential Geometry and Topology.

Data Science

I started in the data science field as an economic analyst at the Getulio Vargas Foundation. Applying mathematics, statistics, and coding to a variety of economic problems involving messy datasets was a fun experience.

Since then, I moved to the United States and attended a data science bootcamp, while also self-teaching myself the latest machine learning algorithms and big data tools.
